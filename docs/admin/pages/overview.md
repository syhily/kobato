---
title: Overview
---

Pages are [static resources](/publishing/) that are not included in channels or collections on the Kobato front-end. They are identical to posts in terms of request and response structure when working with the APIs.

```js
GET /api/admin/pages/
GET /api/admin/pages/{id}/
GET /api/admin/pages/slug/{slug}/
POST /api/admin/pages/
POST /api/admin/pages/{id}/copy
PUT /api/admin/pages/{id}/
DELETE /api/admin/pages/{id}/
```
